Characters in Slippin' Jimmy!

the people behind the hit show!

Marco Patsernak

Marco Patsernak is Jimmy McGills best childhood friend. Marco and Jimmy go on many adventures throughout the show forming a strong friendship. Marco dies of a heart attack in 2002 in his late fourtys.

tremt

tremt is the main antagonist of Slippin' Jimmy. Jimmy McGill has been in lots of dangerous situations because of tremt. This man is an all around villan.

Sister Beth

Sister Beth was a nun that turns evil and possesed and started eating kids left and right. Luckly Jimmy and Marco came to the rescue and make a crucifix out of chewing gum and killed Sister Beth.

chuck mcgill

Chuck McGill is Jimmy McGill's older brother. In episode four, "Speed Fights", Jimmy has to fight a police officer to get Chuck a new radio because his old one broke.

Later in his life he developes a disease that causes him to be allergic to electricity and he gets divorced.

Stoner

Stoner works as a train token attendant at a train station. When Jimmy and Marco need a ride to the comic shop he says: "It's a little late for you to be out. Like, uh, alone, man. Hey, isn't it a school night? When do they do school again?" "Next train's not till like 1:15." "Plenty of time, bro."
